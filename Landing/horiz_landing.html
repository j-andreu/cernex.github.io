Our system uses digital certificates to securely link owners to their products. Advanced encryption keeps customer identities private, and a validation feature ensures only true owners can verify product ownership.

We use serial numbers to link products with authentic certificates issued exclusively by the brand, ensuring legitimacy.

Our tool provides unique product traceability, offering details like warranty, purchase date, and materials. Brands can customize the information stored in each certificate.

Our software prevents data tampering and hacks, ensuring only rightful owners validate certificates. We integrate technologies like MetaMask for unmatched protection.
