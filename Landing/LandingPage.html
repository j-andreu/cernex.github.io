FAQ

1.  Do I need a blockchain to access my certificate?
No. When purchasing a product, users can choose weather they want to store the certificates in their own blockchain wallet or access their portfolio with via email.
2.  I can’t validate the ownership of my certificate after providing my personal details.
The main reasons that can drive errors when validating the ownership of a certificate include :
1. Misspelled words.
2. Different information provided in the purchase and validation processes.
3. Spaces after or before words.
4. Incorrect email or information provided.
5. An illegitimate person is trying to claim ownership over a product.
3.  How can I claim the ownership of my product?
YAfter clicking the “validate” button, users will be required to insert their personal details. These will be used to analyse the legitimacy and ownership rights of the user over the product.
4.  Can I transfer my Digital Product Passport?
No. Currently each certificate (Digital Product Passport) can only be owned by the legitimate owner. However future updates will allow users to transfer them to a new owner.
5.  What happens to my Digital Product Passport if I return my product?
Once a customer returns a product the brand will notify us about it. Your Digital Product Passport will be removed from our system and the user will not be able to access it anymore.
